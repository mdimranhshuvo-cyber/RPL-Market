## Design Benefits of this Layout:
1. **Depth-of-Field Blur (Bokeh):** By blurring the left side, the background feels rich, premium, and alive, but the text you place on top in Next.js will stand out sharp and perfectly readable.
2. **Context-Rich Visuals:** Instead of looking like a basic studio catalog cut-out, these banners show real-world premium settings (clinics, pharmacies, warm nurseries, luxury homes), boosting your brand's credibility.
3. **Perfect Widescreen Balance:** The right-hand focal point keeps the visual weight balanced while the left-hand soft background keeps the layout breathing and clean.
